Dragonslayer


Series: 1981, 2 issue mini-series, 1 magazine collection
Publisher: Marvel

Magazine, collects Dragonslayer (1981) #1-2. Based on the screenplay by Hal Barwoods and Mathew Robbins. Adapted by Dennis O'Neil. Art by Marie Severin and John Tartaglione. When the land falls under the shadow of the last dragon, the people turn to the last surviving wizard for help. When tragedy befalls the kind old magic-user, his quest is passed down to his apprentice, a young man with little knowledge in the ways of sorcery and even less in the ways of battle. Can Galen find the courage and strength to become the world's last "Dragonslayer?!" Based on the classic sword & sorcery movie.

Marvel Comics' official comic adaptation of the 1981 movie "Dragonslayer." In this issue, villagers from the kingdom threatened by a dragon appeal to the wizard Ulrich to help them against the creature. But when Ulrich is killed, it's up to his apprentice Galen to do something in part one of this adaptation. Script by Dennis O'Neil and art by Marie Severin & John Tartaglione.
