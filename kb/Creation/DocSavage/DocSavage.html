Doc Savage


Series: Street & Smith: 30 issues 1933; Marvel: 8 issues 1972; 8 issues 1975. DC: 4 issues 1987; 24 issues 1998; 17 issues 2010

1972: Comic book version of the classic pulp magazine hero Doc Savage. Issue features "The Man of Bronze," part one of an adaptation of the Kenneth Robeson novel of the same name in which Doc and his team try to track down who killed his father. Issue also has a one-page text feature on Doc Savage's team members. Script by Roy Thomas and Steve Englehart and art by Ross Andru and Jim Mooney.
