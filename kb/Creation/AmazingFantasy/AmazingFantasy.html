Amazing Adventures / Fantasy


Series: on-going
Illustrator: Steve Ditko
Writer: Stan Lee
Publisher: 1961 Marvel

Amazing Adventures #1-6, becomes Amazing Adult Fantasy #7-14, then Amazing Fantasy #15, the first appearance of Spider-Man, which became so successful they changed the name to Amazing Spider-Man #1. The series came back for 3 issues as Amazing Adventures #16-18 in 1995.
