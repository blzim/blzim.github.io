Bone


Series: on-going
Illustrator: Jeff Smith
Writer: Jeff Smith
Publisher: 1991 Cartoon Books, Image

Fone Bone, Smiley Bone and Phoney Bone are run out of Boneville and get separated in the desert. Fone Bone finds his way to the valley.

While babysitting Miz Possum's children, Bone is chased by Rat Creatures, is saved by the Dragon and finally finds Thorn.

Issues:
Cartoon Books #1-20
Image books #21-27 (27 issues, re-numbered #1 at issue 22)
Cartoon Books #28-55, 35 issues, making the total 82 issues.
