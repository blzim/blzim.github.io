15 Love


Series: 3 issue mini series
Writer: Andi Watson
Illustrator: Sho Murase, Tommy Ohtsuka
Publisher: 2011 Marvel

About: Originally solicited with Marvel's failed line of faux-Manga (Tsunami) the series was canceled/shelved for many years until it was finally released without the originally planned photo covers.

In 2003, Marvel's then-president, Bill Jemas, told the press there were plans to reimagine Millie the Model as a 15-year-old tennis player for a comic-book series called 15 Love, to be targeted at teenaged girls. Features Millie Collins niece, Misty or Mill Collins. It is set to run for three issues, with each as a double-sized 56-page story.

Story:
Tennis is Mill Collins whole life...but as the lowest ranking student at the Wayde Tennis Academy, she's about to lose her scholarship, and any chance at reaching her dream. What will she do next?
